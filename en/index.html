How OPERA GUARD works in practice
Step 01
Operation Capture
Cameras monitor task execution in real time, without physical contact.
Step 02
Operational Standard
Engineering defines the correct sequence and acceptance criteria for the operation.
Step 03
AI-Based Analysis
Computer vision compares real execution against the expected standard.
Step 04
Decision & Alert
Deviations are identified immediately, before defects occur.
Step 05
Recording & Evidence*
Relevant events are recorded to ensure traceability and support analysis.
